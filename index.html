We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Applications

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Remove my Shame for Chrome, Firefox or Edge. Individual settings relating to the Remove my Shame apps are not personal and are stored only on your device and account. You might also be asked to provide access to your email, but this is only so you can check if your account is marked as paid in Stripe. We don’t process that information at all and have no access to it.

Privacy and 15,000 database.

To keep it simple, we store the 15,000 word list locally in the app. Meaning everything is stored local. No collecting of data, No reading of your history. We just remove your history that matches are list of 15,000 NSFW sites and terms.
